About the Project

Built for Maharashtra
Engineering Aspirants

MAHA-DSE College Predictor is a data-driven tool that helps Direct Second Year (DSE) engineering students in Maharashtra understand which colleges they are likely to get admission in — before CAP counseling begins.

Using official cutoff data from Maharashtra State CET Cell across multiple CAP Rounds and academic years, the predictor analyzes your percentile, caste category, gender, and domicile to return a ranked, categorized list of realistic options.

FAQ

Common Questions

The predictor uses official cutoff data published by Maharashtra State CET Cell for each CAP Round. Accuracy depends on how closely the current year's cutoffs match historical data. Treat results as guidance, not guarantees — actual cutoffs vary each year.

Select your actual caste certificate category. The predictor filters cutoffs that match your category exactly, so incorrect selection will give misleading results. Common options: OPEN (General), OBC, SEBC, SC, ST, NT1/NT2/NT3, EWS.

For DSE (Direct Second Year), the percentile used is derived from your diploma aggregate marks, converted to a percentile by MSBTE/CET Cell. This may differ from your MHT-CET score if you appeared for that exam.
